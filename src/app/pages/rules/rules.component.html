Game objective

The player who first reaches 2000 points wins. Players take turns rolling dice and collecting points for certain combinations.

 Scoring rules
  • 1: 100 points
  • 5: 50 points
  • Three of a kind: Value Ã— 100 (e.g. three 4s = 400 points)
  • Three 1s: 1000 points
  • Four of a kind: Double the points of three of a kind (e.g. four 4s = 800 points)
  • Five of a kind: Four times the points of three of a kind (e.g. five 4s = 1600 points)
  • Six of a kind: Eight times the points of three of a kind
  • Full straight (1-2-3-4-5-6): 1500 points
  • Partial straight (1-2-3-4-5): 500 points
  • Partial straight (2-3-4-5-6): 750 points
 Round procedure
  1. Player rolls all 6 dice
  2. Must select at least one scoring die
  3. Selected dice are set aside and their points are added (round)
  4. Player can choose:
    • Continue: Roll remaining dice. If no points, loses all round points (farkle)
    • End: Take round points and end turn
  5. If player uses all dice, rolls again with all 6 dice (without losing points)
 Farkle

If a player has no scoring dice in their roll (no 1s, 5s, or combinations), they lose all points from the current round and their turn ends.
